Travel & Accommodation

Rhodes is easy to reach from the UK, and we've gathered everything you'll need to plan your stay.

✈ Flights

Direct flights are available from many UK airports including Birmingham, East Midlands, Manchester and London.

🏨 Where to Stay

We recommend staying in Pefki or nearby Lindos for the best beaches, restaurants and easy access to the venue.

We shall Whatsapp you with a list of recommended hotels very soon!

🚖 Transfers

Rhodes Airport is around one hour from Pefki. Taxis and private transfers are available throughout the day however there is also cheap car hire at the airport that works out much cheaper than the taxis.

🌅 Things to Enjoy

Beautiful beaches, boat trips, Lindos Acropolis, sunset cocktails and traditional Greek tavernas.
